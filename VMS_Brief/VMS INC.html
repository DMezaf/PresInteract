About Us

Engineering with ethics and safety

Certified processes, operational leadership, and measurable results.

Not just a vendor: a strategic partner

In a market that prioritizes volume and price, VMS Energy prioritizes the solution. Our origin is not the equipment; it is the challenge of enabling confident growth in industrial operations with safety, efficiency, and ethics.

The Challenge

Rising demand and the need for smarter, more sustainable solutions. For many it is engineering; for us, it is leadership.

The Purpose

Solve complex problems with transparency, a motivated team, and health and safety as non-negotiables.

The Solution

Integrated EPC to solve electrical and mechanical challenges with the right tools.



Who we are

VMS Energy is a Mexican company with more than 15 years of experience in engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC) for electrical systems and industrial lighting. Our multidisciplinary team specializes in industrial, commercial, and infrastructure projects, delivering end-to-end, tailored solutions.

Integrated policy

  • Provide competitive solutions to industrial electromechanical and automation/control challenges, safeguarding the physical and mental integrity of our teams, respecting the environment, optimizing energy consumption, and meeting all regulatory requirements ethically. We are supported by our QHSE+ framework and continuous process improvement driven by effective leadership, people development, and recognition of performance.
Corporate philosophy

Mission

Deliver high-quality sales and services for electric power generation and distribution systems, with more versatile and sustainable solutions for our clients.

Vision

To be a leading EPC solutions company in the Mexican energy market by 2030, contributing to the development of the country.

Values

  • VTruth: We say what is true and act on facts; the foundation of trust.
  • MMotivation: A steady commitment to fulfill agreements with all stakeholders.
  • SHealth and Safety: The priority to execute work without exposing anyone to risk.
  • EEnvironment: Protecting the environment is critical to our business.
  • NIntegrity: No hidden intentions; fair and respectful treatment.
  • EEfficiency: Commitment to responsible energy use and emissions reduction.
What we do at VMS Energy

We deliver tailored engineering, procurement, and construction solutions that are reliable and sustainable, aligned with international quality, safety, and sustainability standards as competitive differentiators. We aim to be trusted, efficient, and responsible leaders.

Core business

  • EPC (engineering, procurement, and construction).
  • Power generation, transmission, and distribution.
  • Electromechanical challenge resolution.
  • Multi-brand automation and control.
  • Electrical grid infrastructure.
  • Oil and gas.
  • Industrial, roadway, and architectural lighting.
  • Renewable energy
  • Specialty supply (MCCs, switchgear, VFDs, regulators, actuators, retrofit, cable, LED luminaires, ATEX, etc.).
How we do it

Just as important as what we do is how we do it: certified processes, documented ethics, safety as a culture, and measurable operational efficiency.

End-to-end reliability and sustainability

  • Process excellence ISO 9001-based approach (Certificate GSC9KMX505)
  • Environmental excellence Environmental management aligned to ISO 14001 (Certificate GSC14KMX277)
  • Ethical commitment Anti-bribery and transparency program aligned to ISO 37001 (Certificate GSC37KMX131)
  • Human well-being as the core of Health and Safety Management under ISO 45001 (Certificate GSC45KMX182)
  • Energy responsibility and efficiency to reduce and optimize electrical consumption under ISO 50001 (Certificate GSC50KMX101)

Regional footprint

Our coverage


Operational coverage across the United States, Mexico, Central America, and the Caribbean. We support EPC projects with logistics, engineering, and on-site support.

  • Rapid response for procurement phases and start-ups.
  • Multi-site coordination for regional operations.
  • Risk management and operational safety (QHSE+).
